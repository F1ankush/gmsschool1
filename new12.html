About Our School

Greenwood High School was established in 1985 with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures young minds and prepares them for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our campus spans over 10 acres with state-of-the-art facilities including modern classrooms, science and computer labs, a library with over 20,000 books, and excellent sports facilities.

We follow the CBSE curriculum with a focus on holistic development through academics, sports, arts, and community service.
